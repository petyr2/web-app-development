Biography

Peter Mucheru is an aspiring cybersecurity professional and telecommunications engineer.

He is currently pursuing Professional Foundation and Cybersecurity training at ALX Africa, alongside a Diploma in Telecommunication Engineering at the Nairobi National Polytechnic.

Positioning himself at the intersection of networking, cybersecurity, and web development, Peter is building a personal brand centered on continuous learning and hands-on practice.
His career goal is to become a trusted cybersecurity expert who helps organizations strengthen digital infrastructure, secure networks, and stay ahead of emerging threats.

Peter has gained hands-on penetration testing experience through TryHackMe labs, built a solid foundation in networking and system security, and applied his knowledge in Flask web development to create practical, security-aware web applications.
He has also contributed to projects that translate theory into real-world results.

With this unique blend of engineering, security, and development skills, he aspires to make technology safer, more reliable, and more accessible for communities and organizations alike.
